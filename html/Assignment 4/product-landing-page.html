Royal Enfield Continental GT-600

The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is a neo-retro café racer that seamlessly blends classic aesthetics with modern performance. Powered by a 648cc parallel-twin engine, it delivers 47.65 PS at 7,150 rpm and 52 Nm of torque at 5,250 rpm, ensuring a dynamic and responsive ride. The six-speed transmission with a slip and assist clutch enhances gear shifts, providing both comfort and control. EN.WIKIPEDIA.ORG Designed for both style and functionality, the Continental GT 650 features a retro-inspired design with clip-on handlebars and a hump seat, embodying the classic café racer spirit. The 13.7-liter fuel tank offers a balance between capacity and the bike's compact profile. The 41mm front fork with 110mm travel and twin coil-over shocks with 88mm travel ensure a comfortable and stable ride across various terrains.
